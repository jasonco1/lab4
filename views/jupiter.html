Welcome to Jupiter!

Jupiter is a gas giant, and the biggest planet in our solar system. It does not have solid surface, has cloud stripes surrounding it, and actually does have rings but they are too faint to see.Jupiter has a thick atmosphere made of mostly hydrogen and helium. It is the fifth planet from the sun and actually has 79 confirmed moons!
