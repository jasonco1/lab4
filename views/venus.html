Welcome to Venus!

Venus is mercury's and Earth's neighbor and is the second closest planet to the sun. Despite Mercury being closest to the sun, venus is the hottest. This is due to Venus's atmosphere being full of greenhouse gases trapping the heat and warming up the surface to about 900 degrees Fahrenheit (465 degrees Celsius); meaning it's surface is hot enough to melt lead and other metals! Venus is an active planet with mountains and volcanoes. It is slightly smaller than Earth but spins in the opposite direction meaning the sun rises in the west and sets in the east. But on Venus the sun rises every 117 Earth days!
